How did they vote?

A Twitter bot to inform how UK MPs have voted on issues.

Why / What is this about?

At the heart of the UK's representative democracy is how our MPs have voted on issues on our behalf. However the decsions made by indiviual MPs can be hard to find and demonstrate to others or the MP themselves. This bot helps to make these decisions clear and easily available.

How to use:

  • Include @howdidtheyvote1 in a twitter message
  • Type an MPs name followed by some punctuation (comma, dash etc)
  • Type the issue you are interested in
  • E.g. @howdidtheyvote1 Liz Truss, Brexit
  • The bot will reply to you next time it runs

How it works

  • For specific issues the voting data is downloaded from the UK parliament website
  • It is formatted and appropriate key words collated for each topic
  • The bot listens out for mentions and if it can interpret the tweet will respond with the relevant data plus a link to the source
